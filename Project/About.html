Welocome To Arctic Fox WiFi Services

Arctic Fox provides high speed data, voice and video services over the Internet.
It primarily provides high-speed Internet services to residential, corporate, and
small business customers. For corporate customers, Arctic Fox offers dedicated leased
lines to allow large amounts of data to be transferred. It was the first private ISP to
announce a 2Mbps plan in 2007, when the government mandated all ISPs to provide service.

One of the few companies that can offer triple-play services alongside major carriers.
This means your customers can access broadband, make voice calls (VoIP) over the Internet,
and watch video over a single connection. Arctic Fox Broadband offers these services to
individual home and business customers.
